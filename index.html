Who I Am

I'm Qammar Abbas, a passionate full stack developer and tech enthusiast based in Lahore, Pakistan. I believe technology should create real-world impact — not just solve problems on screens, but change lives in communities.

My journey started with a deep curiosity about how digital systems work, which led me through frontend development, backend engineering, Python scripting, and data visualization. Today I build end-to-end solutions that blend technical precision with creative vision.

Beyond the keyboard, I serve as Scouts In-charge at Al-Mustafa Markaz, Wahdat Road, Lahore through the Imamia Scout Organization (ISO). Previously I served as Finance Director in 2024. Leadership, community service, and youth development are as central to my identity as any tech stack.

My future goal: build a software company and call center while continuing meaningful welfare work — merging innovation with impact.
